May the Courage Be with you

Hello, I'm Courage, born in 1996 in Linfen, Shanxi, China. I developed a passion for programming initially through algorithm competitions, being the first in my high school to participate in the National Olympiad in Informatics (NOIP) and later the first in my university to join the International Collegiate Programming Contest (ICPC). Currently, I work as a software engineer at Microsoft Suzhou. In my spare time, I enjoy using code to create interesting things 🧑‍💻.

Beyond programming, I'm also an amateur musician (feel free to check out my works on Youtube). I have a love for poetry and share recitations (most are Chinese) on my radio And Yet the Poems. Additionally, I have a fondness for reading 📖, swimming 🏊 and hiking 🥾.

If you want to contact me, welcome to send me an email.
